About Me

Education and Interests

From 2021-2025, I completed my undergraduate degree at San Diego State University. While at SDSU, I studied a variety of topics. Among some of the things that I studied are Algorithms & Data Structures, Computer Networks, Computer Security, Cloud Computing UNIX System Administation, Database Theory & Implementation, and more.
In May of 2025, I obtained my Bachelor's of Science at SDSU. I graduated Summa Cum Laude, and was identified as one of the 2025 Outstanding Computer Science Undergraduates by the department.

This fall, I'll start pursuing my Master's of Science at UC Davis. One of the things that I love about computer science is how diverse the field is. CS spans many topics, and if I could, I'd study them all. However, while pursuing my master's, my topics of focus will be Software Engineering, Programming Languages, and Computer Science Education.
